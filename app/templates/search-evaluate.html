About the service

Learning outcomes must be achievable, relevant, timely, consistent and succinct and clearly understandable by students. Course learning outcomes should avoid passive verbs and phrases such as "Be familiar with" or "Be able to" and including tasks in the learning outcomes. It is important that learning outcome set covers different levels of thinking and knowledge area. With these requirements in mind, our service evaluates learning outcomes of existing courses in UNSW.
