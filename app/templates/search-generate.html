Learning Outcomes Generator

About the service

Our service will generate learning outcome suggestions based on the information associated with a chosen course. This varies from other systems as we process information associated with the course initially before asking more user input. If generated suggestions are too general for the course, individual learning outcomes can also be added. Existing courses are often required to be changed and making adjustments is a manual process that is timeconsuming. With this system, suggestions can be made easily with existing course information. Further, assessments can also be recommended that are more suitable for changed learning outcomes.
